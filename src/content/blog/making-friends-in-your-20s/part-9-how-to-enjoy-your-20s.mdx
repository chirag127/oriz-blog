# How to Enjoy Your 20s Properly

Your 20s are NOT just about:
* Salary
* Career progression at TCS
* Productivity
* Hustle culture

They are also about:
* Experiences
* Friendship
* Love and attraction
* Travel
* Finding your Identity
* Adventure
* Creating stories you'll tell in your 30s and 40s

You want to get the most enjoyment at your age, every time, everywhere. The best way to do that is to embrace a wide range of experiences while avoiding destructive behaviors.

## Things You Should Experience

* **Solo travel:** Take a weekend trip somewhere nearby. It forces you to be independent.
* **Group trips:** Go to the mountains or the beach with colleagues or friends.
* **Fitness transformation:** See what your body is capable of.
* **Deep friendships:** Spend all night talking about life with someone you trust.
* **Dating:** Figure out what you like and don't like in a partner.
* **Night drives:** Drive around Bhubaneswar when the streets are empty.
* **Trying new hobbies:** Pick up a guitar, try coding a game, or learn to cook.
* **Learning social confidence:** Put yourself in uncomfortable social situations until they become comfortable.

## But Avoid:

* Addiction (to substances, screens, or bad habits).
* Reckless hookups that leave you feeling empty.
* Emotional manipulation.
* Toxic friend groups who drag you down.
* Excessive alcohol dependence to feel socially confident.
* Gambling or blowing your hard-earned salary recklessly.
* Self-destruction disguised as “fun.”

Enjoyment comes from building a life you don't need to constantly escape from.
