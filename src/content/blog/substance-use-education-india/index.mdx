# Substance Use Education India (SUEI)
### A 50-Part Clinical & Harm-Reduction Encyclopedia

Welcome to the **Substance Use Education India** series. This project aims to provide transparent, evidence-based, and clinical-grade information regarding medications and substances frequently misused in the Indian subcontinent. Our goal is to reduce harm through education, providing citizens with accurate data on pharmacology, toxicity, and the legal framework under the NDPS Act.
